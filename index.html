Family Trip

Dates: May 18, 2025 - May 24, 2025

Niagara Falls, New York

Hotel: Hotel NY Falls Inn

Hotel Info: Located near the falls, includes breakfast, family-friendly.

Attractions: Maid of the Mist, Observation Tower

  • Maid of the Mist: Boat ride to the base of the falls.
  • Observation Tower: Panoramic views of the falls.

Estimated Cost: $500

Niagara Falls, Canada

Hotel: Fallsview Hotel

Hotel Info: Rooms with a view of the falls, spa and dining included.

Attractions: Journey Behind the Falls, Skylon Tower

  • Journey Behind the Falls: Walk through tunnels behind the falls.
  • Skylon Tower: Observation deck and revolving restaurant.

Estimated Cost: $600

Toronto

Hotel: Toronto City Hotel

Hotel Info: Centrally located, includes access to gym and pool.

Attractions: CN Tower, Royal Ontario Museum

  • CN Tower: Iconic landmark with skywalk experience.
  • Royal Ontario Museum: Natural history and world cultures museum.

Estimated Cost: $700
