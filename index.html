Family Trip

Dates: May 18, 2025 - May 24, 2025

Daily Itinerary

Day 1 (May 18): Arrive at Niagara Falls, New York – Check-in, explore nearby sights.
Day 2 (May 19): Visit Maid of the Mist & Observation Tower.
Day 3 (May 20): Morning in NY, afternoon travel to Niagara Falls, Canada – Journey Behind the Falls.
Day 4 (May 21): Explore Skylon Tower and enjoy falls-side dining.
Day 5 (May 22): Morning in Canada, afternoon travel to Toronto – CN Tower at night.
Day 6 (May 23): Visit Royal Ontario Museum and explore downtown.
Day 7 (May 24): Shopping or sightseeing, prepare for departure.

Niagara Falls, New York

Planned Days: Day 1 to Day 3 (morning)

Hotel: Hotel NY Falls Inn

Hotel Info: Located near the falls, includes breakfast, family-friendly.

Attractions:

  • Maid of the Mist - $25 - Iconic boat ride to the base of the falls
  • Observation Tower - $5 - Panoramic views of the falls

Niagara Falls, Canada

Planned Days: Day 3 (afternoon) to Day 5 (afternoon)

Hotel: Canadian Falls View Hotel

Hotel Info: Offers rooms with views of the falls, on-site dining, pool.

Attractions:

  • Journey Behind the Falls - $30 - View falls from tunnels below
  • Skylon Tower - $20 - Dining and observation deck with skyline views

Toronto

Planned Days: Day 5 (afternoon) to Day 7

Hotel: Toronto Central Hotel

Hotel Info: Centrally located, includes breakfast and access to public transport.

Attractions:

  • CN Tower - $35 - Iconic tower with glass floor and 360 restaurant
  • Royal Ontario Museum - $25 - History and science exhibits
